Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a cystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random number, you lose the game if your score goes above that random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game changes the values of each crystal.
